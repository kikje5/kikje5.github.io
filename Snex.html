Project Overview

This is my first uploaded game, it was made for the GMTK 2023 gamejam which had the theme "roles reversed". Since this was my first game I wasn't very certain of my capabilities and thus focused on just releasing a game. I had previously played around with making snake on a hexagonal grid, and the theme gave me the idea of the player being the apple.
