Project Overview

The theme of the GMTK 2025 gamejam was "Loop". This gave me the idea of needing to make a loop of a trailing rope to do damage. This directed me to a cowboy like theme, which gave me the idea to add a lasso. This resulted in this simple survival game.

My focus for this project

I had a lot more idea's for this game but i didn't have the time to implement them. I only used 1.5 days of the 4, so i focused on just making a game nad getting it uploaded since I did want to participate. I made the choice to do it in MonoGame, since i had been working with it for half a year at the HVA.
